Discovery

Thailand

Ko Lanta

Koh Lanta is a small island laying off the west coast of mainland Thailand in the Andaman sea. It’s known for its scuba diving, white sand beaches. In May to October is considered monsoon season, with strong rains sometimes as frequent as every day. The best time to visit this little gem is November to April.

Dominican Republic

Puerto Plata

Puerto Plata is where tourism in Dominican Republic began. The area boasts beautiful landscapes, relaxed beaches, and lush mountains & valleys. Home to the only aerial tramway in the Caribbean and some of Dominican Republic’s first resorts, there is plenty to discover in beautiful Puerto Plata.
